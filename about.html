Sleepy Duelist

I know nothing about Yu-Gi-Oh! except that the names of cards are kind of funny. After playing a spoken version of this in Discord with friends, I decided to turn it into a game because it's fun.

Just click the button to use sample data, then start generating. It will generate up to 1000 results.
